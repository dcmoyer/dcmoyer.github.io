Coffee shops of the Greater Nashville Metropolitan Area

An incomplete survey of coffee shops in Nashville, TN, and the surrounding area, with particular focus on South Davidson and North Wiliamson counties. Assessments made mostly on opinion and based on how easy it is to work from each place.


The Well Coffeehouse, Brentwood

  • Date: July, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Okay
  • Espresso: ? (Didn't Try)
  • Parking: Good
  • Wifi: Really Bad/Unusable
  • Power Outlets: Some
  • Seating: Good

Notes: Friendly staff, terrible internet. Slow and/or non-existant captive login page.

Just Love Coffee Cafe, Brentwood

  • Date: August, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Okay
  • Espresso: ? (Didn't Try)
  • Parking: Good
  • Wifi: Good*
  • Power Outlets: Good
  • Seating: Good

Notes: Friendly staff, pretty standard coffee, power at the long counters/lower tables. Additional parking in the business park nearby. Wifi requires a Facebook captive-portal login(!), but otherwise stable. Seating may be challenging, but only at peak hours. Overall, a good place to work.

For whatever reason, every sandwich appears to be pressed by a waffle iron, instead of a panini press. Still good, but perhaps a bit buttery.

Fido, Hillsboro West-End/Vanderbilt

  • Date: August, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Good
  • Espresso: Good
  • Parking: Terrible
  • Wifi: Good
  • Power Outlets: Okay
  • Seating: Good

Notes: Friendly staff, good coffee (by Bongo Java), power only at counters as far as I can tell. No parking, and very limited parking nearby. Wifi stable and relatively fast. Hip, I am probably too stuffily dressed.

Barista Parlor, Hillsboro West-End/Vanderbilt

  • Date: August, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Great
  • Espresso: Interesting
  • Parking: Terrible
  • Wifi: Good
  • Power Outlets: Good
  • Seating: Okay

Notes: Great coffee, interesting espresso choices (...espresso and tonic?), power at every table. Seating somewhat limited. Parking the same as Fido across the street, terrible and limited. Wifi also stable and fast. Pretty good peg-board of events. Very Hip, I am definitely too stuffily dressed, but quiet/medium volume music.

The District, Berry Hill

  • Date: August, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Good
  • Espresso: Good
  • Parking: Okay
  • Wifi: Good
  • Power Outlets: Good
  • Seating: Okay

Notes: Good coffee, interesting placement in the "design district" of Nashville, which appears to be a bunch of residential remodelling company offices/studios. Food was microwaved, but still tasty. Wifi was stable, chairs were extant but somewhat uncomfortable. Still a good place to work, even it smells like diesel smog occasionally. Hip, but still, the cafe is in what appears to be a combination flooring/roofing tile warehouse and highway off-ramp, so how hip can you be?

Starbucks on Nolensville Pike, South-East Davidson County

  • Date: August, 2022
  • Drip Coffee: Starbucks
  • Espresso: Starbucks
  • Parking: Great
  • Wifi: Good*
  • Power Outlets: Great
  • Seating: Good

Notes: It's a Starbucks. If you need a clean place to work with AC, this is a good Starbucks. One long table, four or five small tables, some small cushioned chairs, all with power. Wifi requires email address, and permission to spam. Closes late, nearby Prince's Hot Chicken, as well as "Sub-culture Urban Cafe", which sounds interesting but also less work-accessible.
