About Me

I will be joining Vanderbilt University Computer Science in Fall 2022. Please reach out if you want to collaborate! I will be recruiting students/staff for 2023.

I am currently a Post-Doc at MIT CSAIL working with Polina Golland. I was previously a CS grad student at USC under Greg Ver Steeg and Paul Thompson. My research is focused on machine learning and its applications to medical imaging.
